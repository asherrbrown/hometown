Visit Ketchikan

Enjoy history and nature in this vibrant Alaskan town.

Top three activities to do in Ketchikan

Explore Creek Street

The Creek Street Boardwalk housed the town's red-light district until the 1950s and is today home to local shops.

The Salmon Capital of the World

Ketchikan offers many fishing charters, sea kayaking tours, and fishermen's tours. Catch fish and crab or even snorkel in the local waters.

Explore local wildlife

Go hiking or take a floatplane to see black bears, eagles, and more. Don't miss out on this.
